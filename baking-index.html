Recipe 1: Carrot Cake
Ingredients:
  • 230g light brown muscovado sugar.
  • 230g self raising flour.
  • 260g carrots, coarsely grated.
  • 3 eggs.
  • 155ml sunflower oil.
  • 1 tsp baking powder.
  • 1½ tsp ground cinnamon.
  • ½ tsp mixed spice.
  • ½ tsp ground ginger.
Method:
  1. Sift the flour, baking powder and spices into a large bowl.
  2. Add the sugar and grated carrots, then stir until well combined.
  3. Stir in the beaten eggs and oil, then mix well.
  4. Bake at 160°C for 60 minutes or until a skewer comes out clean.
  5. Optional: decorate as desired.


Recipe 2: Sponge Cake
Ingredients:
  • 200g unsalted butter.
  • 200g caster sugar.
  • 200g self raising flour.
  • 4 eggs.
Method:
  1. Cream the butter and sugar together until combined.
  2. Add in one egg at a time, whisking to make sure it is fully incorporated until all eggs have been used.
  3. Whisk in the flour and combine gently.
  4. Bake at 170°C for 40 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. Optional: decorate as desired.




Recipe 3: Red Velvet Cake
Ingredients:
  • 120g unsalted butter.
  • 300g caster sugar.
  • 300g plain flour.
  • 50g unsweetened cocoa powder.
  • 2 eggs.
  • 240ml buttermilk.
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar.
  • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda.
Method:
  1. Cream the butter and sugar together until combined. Add the eggs one at a time whilst mixing, until both are incorporated.
  2. Seive the cocoa powder into the mixture. Slowly seive half the flour to the chocolate mixture, then add in half of the buttermilk. Mix well, then add remaining flour and the last of the buttermilk.
  3. Add two teaspoons of red food colouring gel to the mixture.
  4. In a small mug combine the white wine vinegar and the bicarbonate of soda and then add to the mixture.
  5. Bake at 160°C for 30 minutes. The cake is ready as soon as a toothpick inserted into the middle of the cake comes out clean.




Recipe 4: Chocolate Fudge Cake
Ingredients:
  • 310g caster sugar.
  • 200g self raising flour.
  • 50g unsweetened cocoa powder.
  • 1 egg.
  • 80ml vegetable oil.
  • 190ml milk
  • 190ml boiling water mixed with 2 teaspoons instant coffee powder.
Method:
  1. Combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder in a large bowl. Whisk thoroughly to combine well.
  2. Add oil, egg, vanilla and milk to the flour mixture and beat well to combine. Pour in the boiling water (with the coffee), and mix until glossy.
  3. Bake for about 40 - 45 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the centre of the chocolate cake comes out semi-clean with small amount of cake.
