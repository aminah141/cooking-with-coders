Crispy Sesame Tofu
Ingredients:
  • 280g of Tofu
  • Knob of ginger
  • Sesame Oil
  • Plain or Corn Flour
  • Soy Sauce
  • Spring Onions
  • Basmati Rice
  • Red Chillies
  • Garlic
Method:
  1. Chop your Tofu up into little cubes. Dab cubes with kitchen roll to remove the excess moisture. Place the Tofu cubes into a bowl.
  2. Add 4 tablespoons of soy sauce, one heaped tablespoon of grated ginger, a teaspoon of grated garlic, and a teaspoon of sesame oil. Gently mix together.
  3. Cover the bowl, place it in the fridge and leave to marinate for 15 minutes.
  4. Remove Tofoo from the fridge. Pour a good helping of cornflour into a bowl. One by one, roll each Tofu cube in the flour so they are coated on all sides. By the end, they should all have a gummy layer of flour on each side.
  5. Put on the rice. Start frying the tofu in a pan of oil, turning them until they turn golden.
  6. Garnish with some chopped spring onions and tuck in!




Coconut Cauliflower Coconut Curry
Ingredients:
  • Cauliflower
  • Chickpeas
  • Coconut Milk
  • 2 Limes
  • Red Chillies
  • 2 Brown Onions
  • Garlic
  • 4 Tomatoes
Method:
  1. Finely chop 2 onions and 3 cloves of garlic. Grate a big chunk of ginger. Throw everything into a pan with some oil and let it soften for 5 minutes over a medium heat.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of turmeric, 2 teaspoons of cumin, 2 teaspoons of ground coriander seeds and 2 and a half teaspoons of garam masala. Mix it all together.
  3. Break your cauliflower into little florets, and add that to the pan.
  4. Chop tomatoes and add to pan. Add 600g of chickpeas, can of coconut milk and chili. Mix!
  5. Allow curry to simmer on medium heat for 20mins. Squeeze lime ontop and enjoy!




Chicken and Chorizo Tray Bake
Ingredients:
  • 2 onion
  • Garlic
  • 4 Chicken Thighs
  • 1 Chorizo stick
  • Potatoes
  • Rosemary
  • Lemon
Method:
  1. Chop the onions. Peel the clove of garlic. Peel and chop the potatoes into small chunks.
  2. Turn over to 180 degrees. Chop chorizo into discs about o.5cm thick. Put all ingredients into a tray.
  3. Once all ingredients are in the tray, cover with a good amount of olive oil, sprinkle rosemary and lemon juice, salt and pepper. Whack it in the oven!
  4. Cook any vegetables you would like separately. Cook for around an hour.
  5. Very easy meal and tastes great! Enjoy!




Roasted Beetroot and Goats Cheese Salad
Ingredients:
  • 250g cooked beetroot, cut into wedges
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 100g goats cheese
  • 50g walnuts
  • salad
Method:
  1. Heat oven to 180C and put the beetroot in a roadting tin with 1tbsp oil, 1 tbsp balsamic, salt and pepper
  2. Roast for 2-10mins and then boil in water for 6 mins
  3. For FRESSING add remaining oil and vinegar in a small bowl with seasoning. Add salad leaves and cucumber in and toss together.
  4. Add chopped up cheese and walnuts to roasting tin for a few mins to soften.
  5. Pile leaves, beetroot, cheese and nuts to the plate and drizzle remaining dressing over the top! Enjoy
