Budget Tips

Intro


We all find ourselves at one point or another cooking on a budget, this page will list some top tips for cooking delicious meals on a budget!

From where to shop, to writing and sticking to shopping lists, as well trying to tackle those impulsive buys.

Follow these tips and you'll be saving money in no time.

Good Luck!


Where to shop?
One major aspect of how much money we are spending on our weekly or monthly budget is where we shop.
Being more aware of paying too much for basic items in certain supermarkets is a good way to start to begin to budget.
Make sure to do your research before leaving home and you'll be on track.

How Often?

How often do you go shopping? Do you find yourself doing a big shop and then realising you don't have certain items, so end up having to spend smaller amounts of money but more regularly.

This can lead to higher overall spending, even though it feels like only a couple of pound here and there.

Try to stick to specific days to go shopping and avoid more regular spending.
